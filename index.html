  • A story of editorial excellence and Creative innovation.

    IMS has been meeting the needs of the global business community for up-to-date information on international investment opportunities since 2007. With long-established ties to prestigious media such as FORTUNE, it blends specialized reporting with in-depth analysis and an authoritative editorial overview. IMS reports are targeted at an influential audience of businessmen, politicians, investors, and consumers. Our researchers are based in country, and work on the ground, where they have built up insider knowledge of the trends driving change. We talk to the people who matter, basing our reports on first-hand information with the leading players in business and top government officials, helping foster new trade and business partnerships. Over the last decade, IMS reports have consistently identified the new trends emerging in the global marketplace, supplying our readers with original and pertinent information vital to making the right investment decisions.

  • Exceeding expectation since 2007

    IMS was formed in 2007 in order to meet the communications needs of an ever-increasing global clientele. The world’s growing interest in targeting reporting, business reviews and concerns over the rising competition for global funds led IMS to develop an integrated firm dedicated to providing both world-renowned media and IMS own international client base with a stronger communication outlet. The company’s senior management had already accumulated more than a decade’s experience in the field of special sections when they decided to form the company, and had established strong ties with many of the world’s leading media that they have sustained since, building new relationships along the way.
